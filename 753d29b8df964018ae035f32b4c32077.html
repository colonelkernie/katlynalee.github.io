the different names in SoS

Posted on Thu, Feb 18, 2021 in development

The most common address from the Shulammite maiden to her lover is dod, used 25 times across all eight chapters of the Song. The ESV translates this term to "beloved," but based on the concordance entry of dod, the term looks to be a standard word for "lover." The maiden rarely deviates from using this word to call out to her lover; however, because of such infrequent variation, every other utterance draws heightened attention and marks a significant shift in her relationship with her lover.

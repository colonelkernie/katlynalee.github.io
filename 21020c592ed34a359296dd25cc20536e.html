cleaning ...

Posted on Tue, Mar 30, 2021 food for thought

cleaning

I've always thought about cleaning my stove, knowing that there's probably dust and grime building up in grossness. But I never got around to doing it because cleaning dust that I can't see wasn't as important as the 50 other things I had to get to in my day.

But I decided to cook some pork belly one day and got oil everywhere. Literally just spilled oil accidentally too. That definitely got me cleaning. I not only cleaned my spill, but took that opportunity to clean knobs, the top edge, and all the other places that I never cleaned for my stove.

Even though I knew the stove needed cleaning the whole time, it took a grander mess to compel me to do a thorough cleaning, making it cleaner than it ever was.

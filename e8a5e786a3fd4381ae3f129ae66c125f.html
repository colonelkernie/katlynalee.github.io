cleaning ...

Posted on Tue, Apr 13, 2021 food for thought

cleaning the bathtub today.

Using a large sponge to wipe the walls may be efficient and cover much ground, it misses all the mildew built up in the grout lines.

In order for true cleanliness and purity, blanket statements and strategies won't cut it. Sometimes, you'll need that tiny, narrow brush and scrape the individual lines. And that's what differentiates true cleanliness.
